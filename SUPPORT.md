# Support

## How to get help

- **Questions and general help**: use the support channel documented in [README.md](README.md).
- **Bug reports**: open a [bug report](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.yml).
- **Feature requests**: open a [feature request](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.yml).
- **Security issues**: follow the organization-level `SECURITY.md`. Do not open a public issue for security vulnerabilities.

## What to expect

Maintainers review issues and discussions on a best-effort basis. Response times depend on the project's support model and the maintainer team's capacity.
